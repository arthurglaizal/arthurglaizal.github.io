Eastpak / Flex pak

Brief

Drawing on the brand's know-how in the field of luggage, to enter a sector where products are often old-fashioned and have a feminine connotation, by proposing an innovative model in terms of use and image, for a mixed target of relatively young active people.

Product designed for the Eastpak brand.

Industrial design
  • 2018
  • 4 months
  • University project
  • Laure Kraemer



Research

Benchmark for shopping carts. Analysis of the different products of the Eastpak brand. Concept research. Usage scenario. After thinking about it my partner and I developed a concept where the trolley would be basically a backpack. The backpack is Eastpak's flagship product. Moreover, for a young population to have to invest in a shopping cart that will rarely be used is a brake.




Technical development

The trolley bag is convertible by an accordion system. The telescopic arm stretches the fabric under the backpack to provide more space.





Prototype

A scaled-down prototype was produced to test the feasibility and present the concept.




Final Product

Flexpak, the bag that always serves you. Convertible backpack for the shopping cart. Allows the student to do the shopping on his way home from class.


Arthur Glaizal (2020)
