DAT Foundation

Brief

DATIVE is a startup that offers SaaS software for managing fleets of connected objects. DAT Foundation is the technical and UX/UI design basis for the different products of DATIVE Startup. To create it, I work in collaboration with DATIVE's development team to design and optimize the user experience.

The product is adapted to meet the needs of different sectors: DAT Mountain for ski resort operators, DAT Product for serial equipment manufacturers, DAT Power for energy performance, etc.
